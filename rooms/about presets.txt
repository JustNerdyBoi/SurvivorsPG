Save your rooms presets as .png files named roomtype_variant.png
Example: 1_1.png for 1x1 room variant 1

Use current colors while creating your room:
Walls - black(0, 0, 0) - 1 blocktype
Paths - orange(255, 100, 0) - 2 blocktype
Inner space - gray(100, 100, 100) - 0 blocktype
Loot positions - green(0, 255, 0) - 3 blocktype
Water - blue(0, 0, 255) - 4 blocktype

Outer contour MUST be solid walls
